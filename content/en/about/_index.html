--- title: About CDEvents linkTitle: About menu: main: weight: 10 --- {{< blocks/lead title="About CDEvents">}} CDEvents are a common specification for Continuous Delivery events, enabling interoperability in the complete software production ecosystem.
It's an incubated project at the Continuous Delivery Foundation (CDF). {{< /blocks/lead >}} {{% blocks/section color="light" %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-users" title="Community" %}} The project is currently developed and maintained by the Events Special Interest Group at the CDF. We welcome contributions from anyone! {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-cogs" title="Demos" %}} Our first proof of concept includes CDEvents in Tekton and Keptn. We are building the next PoC. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa-spinner" title="In Progress" %}} Our specification work is driven by interoperability use cases. We are working towards v0.1. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% /blocks/section %}} {{< blocks/section color="white">}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
